Minecraft

Description:


Minecraft (від англ. mine craft — досл. «шахтарське ремесло»)is an open-world first/third-person sandbox video game developed and published by Mojang Studios in 2011. The game started a series of the same name, all of which are characterized by a minimalistic cubic design. The game was developed in 2009 by Swedish programmer Markus Persson, also known as "Notch", and continues to be developed by Markus Persson's company Mojang, which is owned by Microsoft Studios. Minecraft belongs to the games with the widest audience and is recognized as the best-selling video game in history.
