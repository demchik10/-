Gta 5

Description:


Grand Theft Auto V (скор. GTA V) — is a 2013 adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. The events take place in the fictional state of San Andreas, the concept of which is based on southern California. The storyline follows a trio of criminals who are under pressure from a government department and reputable criminal figures. The open world design allows players to freely move around the terrain of San Andreas and the fictional city of Los Santos, based on Los Angeles. The game takes place with a view from the third or first person, and its world is traveled on foot or by transport. Players control three main characters throughout the single-player game and switch between them, both during and outside of missions. The story focuses on a series of heists, and many missions involve shooting and driving.
