Description:


Hearts of Iron IV (укр. Залізні Серця IV)— a video game in the genre of global strategy about the Second World War. Developed by the Swedish company Paradox Development Studio and published by Paradox Interactive. The video game was released on June 6, 2016; the game is a sequel to the Hearts of Iron III video game and continues the Hearts of Iron series. As before, the player has to manage one of the countries of the world, developing its infrastructure, researching new technologies, strengthening the country's armed forces, entering into new treaties with other countries, etc. in order to lead it to victory - not necessarily as it happened historically.
